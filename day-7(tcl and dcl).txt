GRANT AND REVOKE:
================

Create a new admin table.
create a user table.


1) Grant:
   =====

2) REVOKE:
   ======

REVOKE OPERATION(SELECT,INSERT,ALTER,UPDATE,DROP) FROM ADMIN TABLE_NAME FROM USER_NAME;
